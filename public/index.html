About Us

iCareer Climber takes resume information and digests it to be useful information for the job seeker.

The best resources for career guidance today are top 10 articles and static databases. By creating a data pipeline that processes historical career information, we are able to derive key career attributes for the user. Our work has shown promising results. The wisdom of the crowd can in fact be leveraged to guide our users to their next step.
